Wallpaper Fixing

Home / Services / Wallpaper Fixing

Transformative Patterns & Textures

Wallpaper offers an immediate and dramatic way to inject personality, texture, and warmth into any room. Our professional wallpaper fixing services guarantee an immaculate, bubble-free finish, whether you're creating a bold feature wall or wrapping an entire room.

Hanging wallpaper, particularly complex patterns or delicate materials like silk or vinyl, requires extreme precision. Our decorators are experts in pattern matching and seamless joining. We ensure your chosen design flows perfectly across corners and around obstacles, delivering a high-end designer finish.

  • Expert installation of all wallpaper types (Vinyl, Non-Woven, Fabric).
  • Flawless pattern matching and seamless joins.
  • Safe and clean removal of old wallpaper.
  • Wall preparation, sizing, and lining paper installation.
  • Installation of murals and custom photographic prints.

Our Process

Perfect wallpaper starts with a perfect wall. If necessary, we strip old paper, fill imperfections, and sand the walls smooth. We then apply a 'size' to the wall to help the adhesive bond and allow for minor adjustments during hanging. We measure and cut your wallpaper meticulously to ensure the pattern aligns flawlessly across drops. Using premium mold-resistant pastes, we hang the paper, gently smoothing out any air pockets, and precisely trim the edges around skirtings and ceilings.
