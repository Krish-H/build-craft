Home / Services / Plaster Works

The Foundation of Flawless Interiors

Perfect walls are the foundation of any beautiful interior space. Our expert plastering services provide smooth, flawless canvases for paint or wallpaper, ensuring an impeccable final aesthetic for your renovation or new build.

From skimming over damaged walls and ceilings to complete architectural plastering, our skilled tradesmen bring decades of combined experience to every job. We meticulously repair cracks, water damage, and structural imperfections using premium-grade plaster materials that guarantee a durable, long-lasting structure.

  • Precision skimming for an ultra-smooth finish.
  • Expert repair of cracks, holes, and water damage.
  • Installation of plasterboard and drywall partitioning.
  • Decorative plastering, cornices, and coving.
  • Reliable, mess-free applications with sharp corners.

Our Process

Preparation is key in plastering. We first secure and clean the underlying masonry or boards, applying PVA adhesive where necessary for maximum bond strength. Our plasterers then apply layers of base and finish coat plaster, smoothing it continuously to iron out any ripples or trowel marks. We ensure sharp external angles using corner beads and leave the site spotless while the new plaster cures perfectly in preparation for decoration.
